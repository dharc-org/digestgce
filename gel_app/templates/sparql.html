HOW

Query and export GEL data

GEL data are served as Linked Open Data, released under a CC-BY license. Bibliographic data are described according to the SPAR Ontologies.

You can export GEL data from the search interface as .txt or .ris documents (compatible with Zotero, Mendeley and other citation data managment systems).

Alternatively, you can query the data via our SPARQL endpoint GUI or REST API.

https://projects.dharc.unibo.it/digestgel/sparql
