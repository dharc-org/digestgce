Global Education and Learning

An annotated bibliography on global education

The Global Education knowledge base provides an annotated bibliography of academic and research materials relevant to the field of global education, as defined in the Maastricht Declaration on Global Education (2002).

The first and second editions of ANGEL's Global Education Digest included only English-language publications, highlighting the fact that most scholars tend to publish in English. The unequal distribution of publication languages provided the main impetus for the development of the third and fourth edition, which led contributor teams to search and collate literature in 7 further languages, covering material published in English, Dutch, French, German, Italian, Polish, Portuguese and Spanish.
